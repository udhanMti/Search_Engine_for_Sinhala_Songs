Scrape https://sinhalasongbook.com/ to get lyrics and meta-data for 500 Sinhala songs

<B>Meta-data scapped</B>
<li>Artist</li>
<li>Lyric writer</li>
<li>Composer</li>
<li>Song title</li>
<li>Genre</li>
<li>Beat</li>
<li>Key</li>
<li>Number of views</li>
<br>
<B>Tools used</B>
<br>
<br>
<li>Scrapy 2.1.0</li>
<li>googletrans 3.0.0</li>
